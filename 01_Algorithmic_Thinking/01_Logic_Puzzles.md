# Logic Puzzles for Computational Thinking

## Puzzle 1: The Wolf, Goat, and Cabbage
You have a wolf, a goat, and a cabbage. You need to cross a river.
- The boat can only hold you and one other item.
- If left alone, the wolf will eat the goat.
- If left alone, the goat will eat the cabbage.
**Task:** Write the algorithm (step-by-step) to get everyone across safely.

## Puzzle 2: The Broken Robot
A robot can only follow these commands: 'FORWARD', 'TURN_LEFT', 'TURN_RIGHT'.
It is facing North.
**Task:** Write the sequence to move it to a location 3 steps East and 2 steps South.
